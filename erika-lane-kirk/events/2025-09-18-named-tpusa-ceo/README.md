## Summary

Eight days after her husband Charlie Kirk was fatally shot at Utah Valley University, Erika Kirk was named CEO and Chair of the Board of Turning Point USA, succeeding her late husband as leader of the conservative youth activist organization he founded.

---

## Context

Following the murder of Charlie Kirk on September 10, 2025, Turning Point USA needed to determine leadership succession. On September 18, 2025, the organization announced that Erika Kirk would take on both the CEO role and the position of Chair of the Board.

Erika Kirk had been a frequent presence at TPUSA events alongside her husband and vowed to continue his work with the organization.
