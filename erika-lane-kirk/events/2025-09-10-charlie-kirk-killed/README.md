# Husband Charlie Kirk Fatally Shot at Utah Valley University

**Event ID:** `2025-09-10-charlie-kirk-killed`  
**Date:** September 10, 2025  
**Status:** final  
**Severity:** high  
**Verification:** verified  
**Source Type:** document (FBI official statements)  

---

## Summary

Charlie Kirk, founder of Turning Point USA and husband of Erika Kirk, was fatally shot at approximately 12:23 p.m. Mountain Time while speaking at Utah Valley University in Orem, Utah. The shooting occurred during the first stop of his fall "American Comeback Tour." The shooter fired from a rooftop using a high-powered bolt action rifle. A suspect, 22-year-old Tyler Robinson, was apprehended 33 hours later and charged with murder.

---

## Context

Charlie Kirk was conducting a speaking event at Utah Valley University as part of his "American Comeback Tour," which invited students on college campuses to debate political issues. This was the first stop of the fall tour.

At the time of his death, Charlie Kirk was:
- Founder and President of Turning Point USA
- Husband of Erika Kirk (married May 8, 2021)
- Father of two children (daughter, age 3; son, age 1)
- 31 years old (would have turned 32 on October 14, 2025)

## The Shooting

### Method

According to FBI statements:

- Shooter climbed to a rooftop around 12:00 p.m. Mountain Time
- Used a **high-powered bolt action rifle**
- After shooting, jumped from the rooftop and fled
- Left gun and ammunition in a wooded area near the university

### Evidence Collected

- High-powered bolt action rifle (recovered in wooded area)
- Ammunition
- Shoe impressions
- Forearm imprint
- Palm print

## Official FBI Statements

### SAC Robert Bohls (September 10, 2025)

> "On behalf of the FBI, we extend our sincerest condolences to the family and friends of Charlie Kirk."

> "Our thoughts are also with the people who witnessed this traumatic event. We know what you experienced was difficult."

### SAC Robert Bohls (September 11, 2025)

> "Any attack on the First Amendment is an attack on the very foundation of our democracy. That is why we will relentlessly pursue this case until the shooter is in custody."

> "We have recovered what we believe is the weapon used in yesterday's shooting: a high-powered bolt action rifle."

### FBI Director Kash Patel (September 12, 2025)

> "In 33 hours, we have made historic progress for Charlie."

> "This is what happens when you let good cops be cops. The FBI and our partners are proud to stand here today together to bring justice to the family of Charlie Kirk and honor his memory."

> "Lastly, to my friend Charlie Kirk: Rest now, brother. We have the watch, and I'll see you in Valhalla."
